üîç Understanding Your Symptoms

Irregular Periods? It could be due to hormonal imbalances, PCOS, or stress. Tracking cycles and lifestyle changes may help.

Pelvic Pain? It might indicate Endometriosis, Ovarian Cysts, or infections. Persistent pain requires a doctor's evaluation.

Unusual Discharge? If it's thick, smelly, or causes itching, it could be an infection like yeast or BV. Proper hygiene and medication help.

Severe Cramps? Extremely painful periods may be linked to Endometriosis or Fibroids. Pain relief and hormonal treatments can ease symptoms.
